The Best 90's Movies of All Time

A collection of the best movies from the 90s. Unarguably

Twister

A story about tornadoes and cows, wrapped in a love story.
There's a reason you have to pay to rent this movie almost 30 years after its debut.

The Lion King

There's no arguement to be made here. Don't even try.
Nothing captures the childhood of a 90's baby like The Lion King.

Halloweentown

In the spirit of the season, Halloweentown makes the list.
A true classic played on repeat in the cozy comfort of millions of homes annually.
